I fell prey to The Mortal Realms partworks thing.

Someone else’s website is very slow to update their lists, so, here’s my index of what’s in each issue.

ForbiddenPlanet sells the things, lets you pic-and-mix, ships internationally, ships sooner and is cheaper than the producer … when you purchase at least 10 issues at once.1 I’m only putting titles and links on things that are in stock.

The publisher offers a subscription service too which gives out a few freebies, but, I was warned off of them.2
