Issue 08 - Lord-Imperatant and Gryph Hound with STC Medium Base Brush

This is the Stormcast commander within the two larger AoS3 starter sets.

… also called “Lord Important and BirbDogo” …

The STC brushes are some sort of cutting-edge synthetic brush. IIRC most synthetic bristles sucked until about 5/10 years ago when the “good” synthetics started becoming basically identical for “hobbyist” use. If you’re scratching your head at that - just stick with whatever.

… if you’re a vegan or (like me) someone who gets uncomfortable thinking about the effort required to produce and refine natural fibres - life is too short to ignore synthetics baby!

  • Magazine
  • Rules (AoS2) (AoS3)
  • The models are in the two “bigger” starter boxes
    • Harbinger Outpost Element
    • Extremis Outpost Element
  • The brush is on its own Outpost Element
  • The Lord’s Lore
  • The Bird’s Lore

Premiered in the Harrowdeep set for Underworlds opposite da Kunnin Krew - both are now in the “Rivals of Harrowdeep” box with their Underworlds cards. They were part of a “bookstore game” called “WarHammer Quest; Lost Relics” opposed by cardboard tokens for many of the older Underworlds warbands. At the time of writing - they’re the heteronormative warband in the Warcray; Crypt of Blood Starter Set.
