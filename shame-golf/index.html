Just like boomer-golf and mini-golf, miniature-shame-golf is a game of trying to get your score “down” by completing models.

Score Action
-1 Finish a model
+1 Acquire a model
-1 Give away / sell a model
-1 Finish a unit1
-5 Finish a faction box
-20 Finish a “Boxed Game” 2
+5 Acquire box of 5-10 models within season3
+20 Acquire a faction box within season3
+50 Acquire a “Boxed Game” within season3
+1 Each unfinished model present when priming4

2021-06-30 / Bloody Many Dynasty (-1)

The “Bloody Many Dynasty” is my latest new Necron colour scheme to find something that I like “enough.” I have spares to try 19 more times before I need to commit.

If you want to try something like this;

  • This figure (and more) are in the Warhammer 40,000 Recruit Edition
    • … and the “other” starters, with many others - but, those are more expensive
    • … it’s also in teh “Necron Warrior’s” set but I see no reason to recommend that
  • You can get some paints and figures in the Necron Warriors + Paint Set
  • There’s also the “Getting Started” magazine for Â£8

… you can boop me for more detailed paints, but;

  • metallic (sort of equal parts)
    • Game Colour Smokey ink
    • Metal Medium
    • Glaze Medium
  • base
    • misc colours underneath I’m not happy with
      • I’m going to try purples and blues
    • Gloss Varnish to aid crackling
    • Mordant Earth lumped on thick for the crackle
    • Steel Grey drybrushed around the tips

  1. It should be at least 5+ models. I’m ONLY counting a “unit” as “done” if I own no models to “reinforce” it with, and I’m not counting “reinforcements” as a new unit.

    [return]

  2. Two factions and at least 30 figures … or I say “models.” 40k’s “Elite Edition” wouldn’t count as it’s 27 figures, but, “Command Edition” could because of the terrain.

    [return]

  3. Howl says “within 30 days” of release. I’m not counting any partwork magazines but will count “limited” items that’re within 30 days of their release. Otherwise … I say “same season or 30 days” if either applies.

    [return]

  4. I’m worried about minis whose bases scared me or felt “too big to finish.” So I’m trying to avoid leaving unfinished minis (unfinis?) on my desk.

    [return]
